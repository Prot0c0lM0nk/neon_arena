Game Features
Weapon Selection Screen:

Choose from three weapons: Rapid-Fire Rifle, Heavy Shotgun, or Precision Sniper
Each weapon has unique stats for damage, fire rate, range, and ammo capacity
Visual representation of weapon stats with progress bars
Gameplay:

First-person 3D perspective
WASD for movement, mouse to look around, space to jump
Left-click to shoot, R to reload
Visible bullet tracers for both player and enemies
Health system for player and enemies
Progressive difficulty with increasing enemy count each round
HUD:

Ammo counter
Health bar
Round counter
Minimap showing player and enemy positions
Game Flow:

Start screen with weapon selection
In-game action with enemy AI
Game over screen when player health reaches zero
Return to start screen after game over
Technical Features:

Collision detection
Physics for player movement and jumping
Enemy AI with pathfinding and shooting
Particle effects for bullet tracers
3D rendering with shadows and lighting
